Creamed Corn

Description

Creamed corn is pretty much exactly what it sounds like: sweet corn mixed with a creamy sauce. It is often served as a side dish at barbecues or holidays, like Thanksgiving.

Ingredients

  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen corn kernels, thawed
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
