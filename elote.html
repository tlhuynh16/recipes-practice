Elote

Description

Elote is a Mexican street food that consists of corn on the cob covered in a creamy chili lime sauce. The origins of this popular dish trace back to the Aztecs. In fact, the term elote comes from the Nahuatl word for corn.

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of corn
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup cotija cheese
  • 1 lime, quartered
  • Hot sauce
  • Chili powder
